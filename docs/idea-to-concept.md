# Idea to Concept

The first step in AI short drama production is turning a vague idea into a clear concept.

## Inputs

- one-sentence idea
- genre
- target audience
- visual style
- platform

## Concept validation dimensions

- genre clarity
- audience relevance
- story hook strength
- visual feasibility
- AI production feasibility

## Output

A useful concept should include:

- protagonist
- goal
- conflict
- setting
- emotional promise
- short drama potential
