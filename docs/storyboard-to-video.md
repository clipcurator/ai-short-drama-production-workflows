# Storyboard to Video

Storyboard-to-video production works best when the storyboard already defines the purpose of each shot.

## Inputs

- shot list
- character references
- scene references
- camera movement
- duration
- aspect ratio

## Camera movement examples

- pan
- push-in
- pull-out
- tracking
- orbit
- static close-up

## Output review

- character consistency
- scene continuity
- motion quality
- story clarity
- subtitle fit
- episode pacing
